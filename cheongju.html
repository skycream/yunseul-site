An office minutes from the SK Hynix Cheongju campus.

Bokdae-dong sits in central Cheongju with direct access to the M15X and P&T7 corridor. This is where supplier field teams base themselves.

What is being built next door.

SK Hynix runs its NAND and HBM production out of Cheongju, and the campus is in the middle of its largest expansion ever. KTX Osong station and Cheongju International Airport serve the city; the Gyeongbu expressway links it to Seoul in under two hours.

2025

M15X, a new fab built for HBM memory, opened its cleanroom and began equipment installation, on a stated investment of over ₩20tn.

2026

P&T7, a ₩19tn advanced packaging plant, broke ground in April. Air Liquide committed a 200 million euro gas plant to supply it from 2027.

2027 to 2028

P&T7 is scheduled for completion, with around 3,000 operational staff planned on site.

2029

M17, the next NAND fab, is targeted to come online as part of a campus investment plan exceeding ₩100tn.
