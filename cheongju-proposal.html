The building.

Address

Bokdae-dong, Heungdeok-gu, Cheongju. The Gloucester Hotel building, directly opposite Hyundai Department Store.

Building
15 floors + 3 basement

Completed in November 2016. Four passenger elevators and one service elevator.

Unit position
Ground floor

Unit 110, reached directly from the main lobby corridor, with glass frontage on two sides.

Floor area
170.40 m² net (1,834 sq ft)

Exclusive-use area, about 51.6 pyeong. Zoned as a Class 2 neighborhood facility, which admits most commercial uses.

Ceiling height
5.5 to 6 meters

Double-height shell space. Room for a mezzanine, tall equipment, or a showroom volume most ground floors cannot offer.

Building parking

The unit.

A near-rectangular hall of 14.2 by 12 meters with no internal walls: demolition is complete and the space is in shell condition, ready for your fit-out.

Continuous windows run along two sides at street level, and the double-height ceiling gives the room a volume that reads instantly on entry. Labels on the plan are in Korean; a measured plan in English is available on request.

Inside the building.

A hotel above

The Gloucester Hotel operates guest rooms on the upper floors with its lobby, buffet restaurant, seminar rooms and fitness center on the third. Visiting colleagues stay in the same building as your office.

A bank next door

KEB Hana Bank and a pharmacy trade at street level alongside restaurants, bringing steady daily traffic past the unit.

Clinics on the second floor

Medical clinics already operate one floor up, an established pattern of healthcare tenancy in the building.

Managed common areas

Hotel-grade lobby, corridors and elevator halls, maintained by the building, are what your clients walk through to reach you.

The neighborhood.

About 61,000 residents live within 1.5 kilometers, most of them in the apartment complexes that make Bokdae-dong the wealthiest neighborhood in the province.

The retail axis

Hyundai Department Store sits directly across the street, anchoring the strongest retail corridor in central Chungcheong, surrounded by the Jiwell City apartment complexes that set the province's price records.

The employment base

SK Hynix's M11 and M12 NAND fabs operate nearby, with LG Household & Health Care among the district's other major employers. The M15X fab, a 20 trillion won investment, was completed in late 2025.

The demographics

A young workforce city: 73.8% of residents are of working age, and Heungdeok-gu's population is growing, not shrinking.

The terms.

₩100,000,000 deposit

Refundable in full at the end of the lease, per standard Korean commercial practice.

₩5,000,000 monthly rent

Plus VAT. Roughly US$3,600 a month at recent exchange rates, for 170 m² on a department-store corridor.

About ₩800,000 maintenance

Monthly building fee, excluding metered electricity and water. Two years of past maintenance statements are available on request.

Any business type

No restriction on use, subject to the Class 2 neighborhood facility zoning.

Parking included

Two vehicles free. Additional cars park two hours free, then ₩1,000 per hour in the 263-space garage.

Facade signage

A 5.0 by 0.8 meter signage position on the building front, above the bank, facing Daenong-ro.

Two ready-made fit-out studies.

We commissioned a specialist medical-clinic designer to draw two example layouts for this exact unit, compliant with Korean medical facility standards. They are yours to use, adapt or ignore.

Only four internal medicine clinics operate within a 1 km radius of the unit, in a catchment of over 60,000 residents.
